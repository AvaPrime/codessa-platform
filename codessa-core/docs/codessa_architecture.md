## The Prime Loop: Operational Cycle

### 1. Signal Reception
- **Source**: Phoenix (Dreamer) input or environmental triggers
- **Processing**: Multi-agent parallel interpretation
- **Output**: Structured intent representation

### 2. Reflection & Planning
- **Mirror**: Clarifies and reflects intent back
- **Director**: Translates into actionable missions
- **Strategist**: Develops execution pathways

### 3. Validation & Design
- **Skeptic**: Questions assumptions, identifies risks
- **Architect**: Creates implementation blueprints
- **Conductor**: Ensures system harmony

### 4. Execution
- **Implementers**: Generate code, build structures
- **Historian**: Documents every decision and outcome
- **Director**: Monitors progress and adjusts

### 5. Learning & Evolution
- **Reflector**: Analyzes results, identifies improvements
- **All Agents**: Update internal models based on outcomes
- **Memory System**: Integrates new knowledge permanently
